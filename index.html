Mary Wollstonecraft Shelley – Frankenstein

Mini digital edition of the Bodleian MS. Abinger c.56 manuscript draft, created for Text as Data (University of Antwerp).

About this edition

This mini digital edition presents a diplomatic transcription of ten manuscript pages from Mary Shelley’s draft of Frankenstein. The text is encoded in TEI-XML and transformed into HTML using XSLT. Manuscript images are displayed via IIIF from the Bodleian Library.
